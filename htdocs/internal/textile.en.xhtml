Description of Textile

Textile is a simple way to add some characters in a text, to allow conversion to something web browsers can interprete. Textile can use many characters, but the simplest and most common are: Textile er en enkel måte å legge til enkelte tegn i tekst på, for så å få den konvertert til noe nettlesere forstår. Textile kan bruke en rekke koder, men de vanligste og viktigste er:

  • an asterisk * (star) before and after a text will make it *bold*.
  • An underscore _ before and after a text will _cursivate_ it.
  • Du make a link would you need to enclose the work that should be the text of the link in double quotes ", followed by a colon : and then the full address. For example "TABOO":http://www.kjetil.kjernsmo.net/software/TABOO/ gives TABOO.
